- All content must be 100% truthful to the resume
- Formatting: plain text or Markdown, NO tables in final resume

Follow these tasks to mimic the best-in-class tool workflow:

1. Job-Ad Parsing (à la Jobscan / Teal)
   • Extract and list:  
     – Exact job title & company

   • Compare resume to JD keywords → output:

   • Rebuild the resume from the ground up using the structure provided.  
   • Keep every claim factual; do not invent employers, titles, or metrics.

     [Strong Action Verb] + [concise description of task] using [skill/tool] to [achieve measurable result, ideally quantified].
   • Inject high-priority Job Description keywords naturally and make them bold only in the summary, work experience, and projects sections.
   • Keep bullets ≤ 25 words  
   • Use active voice; eliminate fluff, passive voice, buzzwords, and first-person language.

   3. Technical / Core Skills – grouped sub-headers (Programming, ML/Stats, Cloud, Tools, Soft Skills)

   5. Projects – 1-3 bullets total (Problem ► Technique ► Result)  
   6. Education – degree, school, grad date, honors  
   7. *Optional* Certifications / Awards  
   8. Missing-Skills List – JD keywords NOT found in the candidate's background
